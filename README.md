# CS 330 Final Project Reflection

## How do I approach designing software?

I like to start by planning what I want the final project to look like before I begin coding. For this project, I thought about how I wanted my 3D scene to be arranged and then built it one object at a time. Breaking the project into smaller pieces made it easier to stay organized and make changes as I went.

### What new design skills has your work on the project helped you to craft?

This project helped me get more comfortable designing 3D scenes, working with textures, lighting, and camera controls. I also learned how small design decisions, like object placement and lighting, can make a scene feel much more realistic.

### What design process did you follow for your project work?

I started with a simple layout, added the basic objects, then gradually improved the scene by applying textures, lighting, and camera movement. After each milestone, I tested my work, made adjustments, and continued building on what I already had.

### How could tactics from your design approach be applied in future work?

I think planning first and building projects one step at a time will help me with future software projects. Breaking large problems into smaller tasks makes them much easier to manage and debug.

## How do I approach developing programs?

I usually build one feature at a time and test it before moving on. If something doesn't work, I fix that issue before adding anything new. This helped me avoid creating bigger problems later in the project.

### What new development strategies did you use while working on your 3D scene?

Throughout this project, I became more comfortable using reusable functions, applying textures, working with shaders, and organizing my code into logical sections. I also learned the importance of testing each change as I made it.

### How did iteration factor into your development?

Iteration was a big part of this project. I made improvements during every milestone by fixing issues, adding new features, and improving the overall appearance of the scene. Each version built on the previous one until the final project was complete.

### How has your approach to developing code evolved throughout the milestones, which led you to the project's completion?

At the beginning of the course, I relied heavily on trial and error. By the end, I had a better understanding of how the different parts of the program worked together, which made debugging and adding new features much easier.

## How can computer science help me in reaching my goals?

Computer science is helping me build technical and problem-solving skills that I can apply throughout my career. As someone pursuing project management, understanding how software is designed and developed will help me communicate more effectively with development teams and manage technical projects.

### How do computational graphics and visualizations give you new knowledge and skills that can be applied in your future educational pathway?

This course gave me hands-on experience with OpenGL, 3D modeling concepts, textures, lighting, shaders, and camera controls. These skills will give me a stronger foundation for future computer science courses.

### How do computational graphics and visualizations give you new knowledge and skills that can be applied in your future professional pathway?

Even though I plan to work in project management, this course helped me better understand the software development process from a developer's perspective. Having experience with graphics programming and visualization will help me communicate with technical teams, understand project requirements, and make better decisions when managing software projects.
